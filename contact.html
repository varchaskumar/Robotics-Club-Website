Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Robotics Club?

Recruitment drives are held at the beginning of every academic year. Watch out for announcements on our social media pages!

Do I need prior robotics experience?

Not at all! We look for enthusiasm and a willingness to learn. We provide workshops to teach you everything from scratch.

Can students from non-ECE/Mech branches join?

Absolutely! Robotics is multidisciplinary. We need coders, designers, and managers from all branches.
